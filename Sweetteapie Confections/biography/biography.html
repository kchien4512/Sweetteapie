Biography

Hi there! I’m Angela, the heart behind @sweetteapie_confections; a mom of two teenage boys, wife and a full-time occupational therapist working in physical rehabilitation.

Sweetteapie Confections is named after my boys, my “sweetie pies,” the cherished moments spent in the kitchen, and inspired by my love for tea. Embracing my Chinese-Malaysian heritage and love for travel, we create tea-infused treats that are perfectly crafted to pair with your favorite tea or coffee.

Everything is made in small batches, crafted with heart to spread joy. Join me on this journey to spread JOY, one treat at a time. We hope you enjoy our confections and thank you for being a part of our story!
