Frequently-Asked-Questions

What is the shelf life and best way to store? ▾

Our products are handcrafted in small batches and individually packaged to preserve freshness.

CLOUD NOUGATS — We recommend consuming by the best-by date or within four weeks of production date. No need to refrigerate. Store in a cool and dry place away from sunlight.

DUBAI CHOCOLATES — Our fresh pistachio and kataifi filling can be sensitive to heat and humidity. If not consumed immediately, please store in refrigerator in airtight packaging and avoid moisture exposure. Before serving, allow it to sit at room temperature for 10–15 minutes to restore ideal texture. When properly stored, it can last up to two weeks in the fridge.
