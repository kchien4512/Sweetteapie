What is a cloud nougat?

A cloud nougat is our unique twist to Asia's snowflake crisp—a delightful blend of cookie and nougat. These milky confections are soft with an added chewy crunch. Lightly sweetened and loaded with organic tea, nuts, dried fruit, crackers, they offer a healthier indulgence. Best of all, they won't stick to your teeth!
